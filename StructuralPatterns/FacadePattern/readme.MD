### 2. Services

| Service             | Responsibility                                                  |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `InventoryService`  | Maintains product list and stock level                          |

### 3. Facade Layer

- **`CheckoutFacade`**
    - Acts as a unified interface that:
        - Validates stock
        - Processes payment
        - Reduces stock
        - Clears cart
    - Hides complexity of individual services
